Interactive Adsorption Dynamics

Welcome! This interactive visualization helps you explore the adsorption behavior of different materials over time. If you are not familiar with adsorption:

  • Adsorption is a process where substances (like dyes or pollutants) attach to the surface of a solid material (adsorbent).
  • The x-axis represents time in minutes.
  • The y-axis shows the amount of substance adsorbed (qt) in mg per gram of adsorbent.
  • Colored lines represent different adsorbents and how much they adsorb over time.
  • Rectangles represent the adsorbent beds; they blink when they reach 95% of their adsorption capacity.
  • You can use the "Play" and "Pause" buttons in the animation to control the speed.
